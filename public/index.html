TrackEn is a SaaS based voice analytics AI which is emotionally intelligent and is equipped with the latest, state of the art Natural Language Processing for contextual computing. It performs live emotion detection, content analytics and provides in-call suggestions to users for enhanced persuasion and problem solving efficiency. TrackEn gains intelligence from your previous conversations and can pull out deep insights about your businesses, customers and markets.

Trio is ideal for in-call deployments as it provides live, on the fly voice and content analytics. It classifies voice into 3 sentiments- Positive, Negative and Neutral and provides a host of deep insights into conversation with your clients. Get real time suggestion during your conversations and increase your win rates.
